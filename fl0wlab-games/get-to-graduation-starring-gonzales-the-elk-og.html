Get to Graduation! Starring: Gonzales the Elk (Original Release)

Release Date: 5/20/2024

It's graduation day! Vesp, Gonzales' best friend, waits for him. Will you make it to the graduation ceremony before time runs out?

Originally developed for the May 2024 W.E.L.K. Media Senior Memorialization event.

(Congratulations! You found the original version of the game! How'd you find this?)

Controls: Z key to jump, X key to shoot pencils (if applicable), the arrow keys to move Gonzales, and the enter key to start game (You may also use the S or L keys to play in full screen.)

Credits: TheGamer2000 (Lead, Programmer, Tester, Art, Sound), KingKong (Beta Testing), Dani (Beta Testing), Coinen (Beta Testing), Void being (Gonzales Character Ownership and Special Appearance), In association with W.E.L.K. Media
