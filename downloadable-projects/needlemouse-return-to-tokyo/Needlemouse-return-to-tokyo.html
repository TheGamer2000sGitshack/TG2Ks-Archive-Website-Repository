NEEDLEMOUSE: RETURN TO TOKYO

Lead / Developer(s): Dawid, Needle Team

Project Description

Needlemouse needs to save his animal friends from the evil Dr. Eggman! Traverse through 6 zones and collect the Chaos Emeralds to save the day!

This ROM hack by Needle Team is an attempt at reimagining Sonic the Hedgehog with early concept material.

Note that this ROM hack has been released in an incomplete but playable state. It is also open source.

You must have a Sega Genesis/Mega Drive console or emulator to play this project.
