Get to Graduation! Starring: Gonzales the Elk

Release Date: 5/20/2024 (Original), 10/13/2024 (Public)

It's graduation day! Will you get Gonzales the Elk to the graduation ceremony before time runs out?

Controls: Z key to jump, X key to shoot pencils (if applicable), the arrow keys to move Gonzales, and the enter key to start game (You may also use the S or L keys to play in full screen.)
