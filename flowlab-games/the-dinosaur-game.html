The Dinosaur Game (Flowlab Port)

Release Date: 8/28/2025

A Flowlab port of Google's iconic Dinosaur Game. Jump over cactuses and dodge the pterodactyls!

Controls: Z key to jump/start game/reset and the shift key to pause the game/hard reset. (You may also use the S or L keys to play in full screen.)

Credits: TheGamer2000 (Rendition Lead, Programmer, Tester), Google (Original Dev and Assets)
