Sonic the Hedgehog: Flowlab Edition

Release Date: 8/1/2024

Play as Sonic in his first title, (Partially) ported over to the Flowlab engine! Travel through Green Hill Zone as you collect rings and smash motobugs!

Controls: Z key to jump, the left and right keys to move Sonic, the up key to look up, the down key to crouch/curl up, and the enter key to start game. (You may also use the S or L keys to play in full screen.)

Credits: TheGamer2000 (Rendition Lead, Programmer, Tester, Sound), Sega and Sonic Team (Original Game & Assets)
